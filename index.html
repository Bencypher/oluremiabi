PROFILE

Abilawon Oluremi Solomon is a Logistics and Maritime professional with a passion for Maritime Administration, Logistics and Supply Chain Management. With 20 years of experience, Oluremi has established himself as an expert, leader and innovator in Demand Planning and Forecasting, and Sustainability and Green supply Chain. Oluremi specialized in International Trade and Customs Compliance, Supply Chain Security and Compliance, Logistics and Supply Chain Analytics, and Supply Chain Visibility and Tracking.

Business

Far Maritime Logistics and Services Nigeria Limited

Far Maritime Logistics and Services Nigeria Limited is Nigeria's indigenous maritime company, primarily charged with the responsibility of training all levels of manpower through workshops, seminars, and boot camps. We perform shipping operations, logistics, and supply chain management, Maritime News, Tourism, and business opportunities, and other marine-related industries. We operate within the regulations, protocols, and conventions guiding the maritime space nationally and internationally. We are driven by our Mission, vision, and core values. We are gender sensitive and an all-inclusive company. The company is licensed by NIMASA, Nigerian Port Authority, and Nigerian Customs Services to perform and provide shipping and port services to importers and exporters, and other ancillary services at Nigerian Ports In Nigeria. The Company has 10 departments and 2 units to holistically serve prospective clients. We are out to deliver seamless customer services rooted in ethical and conventional practices.

Brief History

Far Maritime Logistics and Services Nigeria Limited is an indigenous maritime and logistics company with N25 Million share capital, registered on 23rd January, 2022 by the CAC of Nigeria (RC1884780) with a focus on water transportation, shipping operations, logistics/supply chain management, and training maritime workers and students. It was founded to change current narratives about Nigeria’s maritime space and its operations.
